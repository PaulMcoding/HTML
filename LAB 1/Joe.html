About Fortnite

Fortnite is a popular online multiplayer game developed by Epic Games. It is available on multiple platforms including PC, Xbox, PlayStation, and Nintendo Switch.

Gameplay

Fortnite is a battle royale game where players drop onto an island and fight to be the last one standing. Players can also build structures and defend themselves against other players.

Items and Weapons

Fortnite features a variety of weapons and items that players can use to defeat their opponents. These include guns, swords, and explosive items.
